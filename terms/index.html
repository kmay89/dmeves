Terms of use

What this site is

This is a personal, unofficial companion website for one teacher’s science class — a visual map that links students to the class’s real materials. It is created and maintained by the teacher as an individual. It is not a website of, endorsed by, or operated by any school, school district, or the State of Ohio, and nothing here speaks for them.

Who it’s for

The site exists for the students and families of this class. The door code is shared in class; please don’t republish it. There’s nothing secret behind the door — just class links — but keeping it among the class keeps the site tidy and its audience clear.

The actual class materials live elsewhere

Assignments, grades, due dates, and official communication happen in Google Classroom and the school’s official systems. If this site and Google Classroom ever disagree, Google Classroom wins. This site is a memory aid, not a system of record.

No warranty (said kindly)

The site is provided as-is, free, for learning. It’s built with care, but it may contain errors, break, or go away. Science facts shown here (element data, planet facts) are simplified for teaching. Don’t rely on this site for anything where an error would matter more than a raised eyebrow.

Your stuff, their stuff

Linked services (Google Classroom, Google Docs, etc.) belong to their owners and have their own terms. The room’s art and code are the teacher’s own work; feel free to enjoy them here rather than republishing them.

Questions
